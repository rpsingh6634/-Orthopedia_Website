Who We Are

Verywell Health is an award-winning online resource for reliable, understandable, and up-to-date health information on the medical topics that matter most to you. We take a human approach to health and wellness content—a welcome alternative to hyper-clinical health sites.

For more than 20 years, we’ve worked hard to provide you with comprehensive content that you can rely on. Our library of more than 18,000 pieces of content has been written by more than 100 healthcare professionals and industry experts including experienced doctors, nurses, and patient advocates, and is vetted by our panel of board-certified physicians. We provide content you can trust so you’re confident in your next steps to better health.

The Verywell family of brands, including Verywell Health, Verywell Fit, Verywell Family, and Verywell Mind, is a top 5 health publisher, according to comScore, a leading internet measurement company, and helps more than 30 million people each month to feel better and be healthier.

Medical Review Board

Verywell’s Medical Review Board is a team of board-certified doctors who ensure our content is medically accurate and reflects the latest in health information.

Our experienced board of medical professionals verify that our content is comprehensive, so you can trust the information you read here will help you through the next steps in your health journey—whether that’s for yourself or for a loved one.

Fact Checkers

Verywell’s team of experienced fact checkers provides a critical layer of review to ensure content integrity. This group of professionals is selected and vetted for journalistic and subject matter expertise. They are tasked with researching all statements of fact to ensure that news and health information articles are accurate and comprehensive and statistics are properly sourced and up to date.

Writers and Policies

When it comes to your health and wellness, you need to feel not only confident about the choices you make, but the information that helps you make them. That’s why we work with writers who are experts in their areas, from board-certified doctors to credentialed dietitians to licensed therapists and more.

Writers are specifically selected for both their extensive knowledge and real-world experience as well as their ability to communicate complex information in a clear and helpful way. Articles also undergo review from our Medical Review Board and routine updating to ensure reporting is responsible, accurate, understandable, helpful, and reflective of the most current research. Read more on our editorial process, including our commitment to diverse voices and anti-racism.

What Our Readers Say

Verywell’s product review team exhaustively researches and recommends a broad range of products and ultimately make a curated list of recommendations from writers with subject-matter expertise in every product category we cover. We do receive an affiliate commission on some, but not all, of the products that we recommend if you decide to click through to the retailer site and make a purchase.
